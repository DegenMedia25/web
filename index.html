How to Buy a Token on Moonshot Using a Solana Wallet

Ready to dive into the exciting world of Moonshot tokens on Solana? Follow these steps to purchase a token directly from the bonding curve using your Solana wallet. The bonding curve ensures a fair price that increases as more tokens are bought—get in early for the best deal!

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Set Up Your Solana Wallet: Download and install a Solana-compatible wallet like Phantom or Solflare. Create a new wallet or import an existing one using your seed phrase. Make sure to back up your seed phrase securely.
  2. Fund Your Wallet: Transfer SOL (Solana’s native cryptocurrency) to your wallet. You can buy SOL on exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or KuCoin, then withdraw it to your wallet’s public address.
  3. Connect to Moonshot: Visit the Moonshot platform (e.g., moonshot.io). Click “Connect Wallet” and select your Solana wallet. Approve the connection in your wallet app.
  4. Find a Token: Browse the “New” section on Moonshot to discover tokens still in their bonding curve phase. Check the bonding curve progress to see how much of the supply has been sold—early buys mean lower prices!
  5. Select Your Token: Click on the token you want to buy. You’ll see details like the current price, market cap, and curve progress. Make sure it’s still in the bonding curve (before it migrates to Raydium or Meteora).
  6. Enter Purchase Amount: Input the amount of SOL you want to spend. Moonshot will calculate how many tokens you’ll receive based on the current bonding curve price. Adjust slippage settings if needed (e.g., 10% default) to account for price changes.
  7. Confirm the Transaction: Click “Buy” and review the transaction in your wallet. Approve it by signing with your wallet. Wait a few seconds for the Solana blockchain to process it—transactions are lightning-fast!
  8. Check Your Tokens: Once confirmed, your tokens will appear in your wallet. You can hold them, trade them later, or wait for the token to migrate to a DEX like Raydium for potential profits.

Pro Tip: Tokens on Moonshot’s bonding curve can skyrocket in value as they approach the migration point (around 500 SOL market cap). Timing is everything—join the Solar System Society and seize your chance at cosmic gains!
